Our Story

Founded in 2015, Burger Haven began as a small food truck with a big dream - to serve the most delicious, high-quality burgers using locally-sourced ingredients. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved local institution.

Today, we continue to honor our roots by crafting each burger with care, from our house-made buns to our special sauce recipes passed down through generations. We believe in food that makes you feel good - in every sense of the word.
